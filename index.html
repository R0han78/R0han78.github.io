Basic Info About Me

My natural knack towards computers and Mathematics, and my capacity for hard work has helped me to outshine in all academics areas. The Integrated M. Tech program at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), provided me with a strong foundation of both Mathematics and Computer Science whilst exposing me to various fields in these domains. My combined knowledge of Mathematics and Computer Science has always given me an edge over the rest when it comes to problem solving. It helps me think analytically and pragmatically.

My current college research project is on Mathematical Modeling of Viruses in a computer Network. Under the guidance of Prof. Dr. R.K. Upadhya, I have studied and analyzed the real viruses distribution models in real humans and also proposed a similar model for the viruses present in computer networks. I realized that real viruses and computer viruses share many common properties. Therefore, in using Mathematical Models on real viruses and integrating it with the concept of Machine Learning, I created a program that could provide a simulation of viruses spreading across a computer network. Analyzing previous spreads of viruses, the program could readily predict which computer node in the network has the highest probability of getting affected next in an already virus infected network. Using this information, virus preventive measures can be conceptualized and implemented, in order to prevent further virus spread in the network.
